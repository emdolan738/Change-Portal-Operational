Strategic Change Designer

Projects do not come in as open requests. They usually arrive through PMO assignment, then the change hub triages them into Gold, Silver, or Bronze support.

Step 1 of 4
Why this intake exists
This is the front door for the change hub. It captures the project context, lets the team assign the right tier of support, and gives Bronze projects a self-service path inside the portal. The goal is to make change visible, comparable, and measurable before it launches.
